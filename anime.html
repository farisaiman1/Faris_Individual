Fighting Review

The reason I like anime so much is because of their fighting scene where the character in the anime have power that normal humans don't have. Some anime character are too OP and they will not die. Example, Naruto and Sasuke from 'Naruto Shippuden'. So in this section, I will list some fight scenes that catch my attention.

1.Naruto and Sasuke vs Momoshiki

This scene is lit! The way Naruto and Sasuke fight to defeat Momoshiki are masterpiece. Overall my rate for this scene is 8/10.

2.Levi vs Beast Titan

In this scene it shows how powerful Levi kill several titan alone and almost kill Beast Titan the leader of titan. With his skill and experience in using Omni-directional mobility gear. Overall my rate for this scene is 6/10.

3. Deku vs Todoroki

A battle between Class 1-A students Izuku Midoriya and Shoto Todoroki in the second round of the U.A. Sports Festival's Fighting Tournament. Overall my rate for this fight is 6/10

4. Saitama Vs Boros

A battle between professional hero and galaxy bandit that really amazing is the reason why this fight is in my list. The hero is too OP where there is no one can defeat him. Overall my rate for this fight is 7/10.
